About Jacobs Music

Jacobs Music is the creation of British guitarist Richard Jacobs.

Starting guitar at the age of 13, since then he has performed in a number of bands across Europe that have required him to play a variety of genres.

Playing in a breadth of genres, brings tons of experience, knowledge and advice to share.

Richard graduated from The Academy of Contemporary Music in 2013, then in 2012 he started teaching at The Hurst Community College and other educational organisations, in which has now led him on to running his own teaching business, Jacobs Music.

As well as running Jacobs Music he was previously a member of the UK Metal band Employed to Serve.
